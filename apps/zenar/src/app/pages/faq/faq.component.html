General Information

What is ZENAR?

ZENAR is a service that protects devices from unexpected damage, including software failures. ZENAR offers guaranteed quick refund of 60-70% of your device’s original value, with no need for lengthy evaluations or repairs.

What types of devices can be covered under ZENAR protection plans?

ZENAR protection plans cover smartphones, tablets, laptops, wireless headphones, smartwatches, small household appliances, and large household appliances.

When does my coverage start?

Protection begins as soon as you purchase the service.

How long does the protection last?

Can I buy ZENAR protection after purchasing a device?

Yes, ZENAR protection can be purchased within three months of buying your device. Visit our Coverage Plans page to select your plan

How do I register for ZENAR protection?

Your ZENAR protection is registered automatically when you purchase the service.

Can I purchase protection for a device not bought from a ZENAR partner?

Yes, you can purchase protection directly from our website. Visit our Coverage Plans page to select your plan

What is the difference between ZENAR and an extended warranty?

Unlike extended warranties, which often involve lengthy evaluations and repairs, ZENAR offers quick refunds of 60-70% of your device's original value. This allows you to buy a new device right away without waiting for repairs or assessments.

I already have the manufacturer’s guarantee. Why do I need ZENAR?

Manufacturer warranties typically cover limited issues and can involve long repair processes. ZENAR, however, covers all types of damage, from liquid spills and drops to software malfunctions. Instead of waiting for repairs, ZENAR offers a fast refund of 60-70% of your device’s original value, so you can buy a new device without the hassle of evaluations or repair delays.
