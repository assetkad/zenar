About Us

Welcome Message from the Founder

Askar Karimov

ZENAR Founder and CEO

Thank you for choosing ZENAR. We're here to support you and protect your devices, so when life’s little accidents happen, they don't get in the way of what matters most to you.
In today’s busy world, we know how essential your devices are for staying connected, getting work done, and enjoying life. That’s why we created ZENAR, a solution that takes the stress out of dealing with broken or damaged devices. It all began over ten years ago when we launched as Keep Talk, a small startup with a big dream: to make device protection easier and more accessible. We knew that traditional warranties often involved complicated paperwork, long waiting periods for repairs, and uncertainty.
We wanted to change that and offer something different: a quick, straightforward guaranteed refund process that gets you back on track in no time. As we grew and evolved into ZENAR, our commitment to innovation and customer satisfaction only strengthened. We've developed a unique service that stands out with a 70% refund guarantee and processing claims in just 5 minutes. This means you can quickly replace your damaged device without the usual stress and delays.
Our story is about more than just growth; it's about the relationships we've built with our customers and the trust they've placed in us. We've become a reliable source of support for over 1 million users, offering peace of mind to our expanding community.
